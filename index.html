Kicker Rules
Kicker: The highest unmatched card breaks ties.
Example: A♠K♦ vs A♥Q♣ on A-7-3 board → AK wins (K kicker > Q kicker).

No kicker used: Straights, flushes, full houses, quads all use all 5 cards.

Playing the board: If the best 5-card hand is the community cards, the pot is split.

🎯 Polarization vs Condensed Ranges
Polarized range = strong value hands + bluffs, no medium strength. Used by aggressor on later streets. Bet large (≥ 2/3 pot).

Condensed range = medium-strength hands, few nuts or air. Used by caller who checked. Vulnerable to big bets.

Merged range = all medium+ hands, thin for value. Bet small (≤ 1/3 pot), high frequency. Good on dry boards IP.
